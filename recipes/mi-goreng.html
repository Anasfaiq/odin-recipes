Indonesian Fried Noodles (Mie Goreng)

Description

Mie Goreng is a delicious and comforting fried noodle dish that’s quick to make and bursting with flavor. It combines chewy noodles, savory sauce, vegetables, and proteins like egg or chicken. Often sold by street vendors across Indonesia, it’s the perfect blend of sweet, salty, and slightly spicy goodness.

ingredients:

  • 2 packs of instant noodles (or egg noodles)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, sliced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (like cabbage, carrot, or bean sprouts)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok, sauté garlic and shallots until fragrant.
  3. Add egg and scramble it quickly.
  4. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Toss in the noodles, soy sauces, chili sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir-fry everything together for another 3–5 minutes on medium-high heat.
  7. Serve hot with fried shallots and cucumber slices on the side.
